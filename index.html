About This App

A future-focused budgeting tool built around clarity, guidance, and financial confidence.

App Information

Simple mission, version, and support details.
Mission Help users understand what is happening with their money now, what is coming next, and what they can do to improve their financial future instead of only reviewing the past.
How to Use This App 1. Set up your profile
2. Add income + paycheck schedule
3. Enter recurring bills + expenses
4. Build your monthly / biweekly budget
5. Set emergency fund + savings goals
6. Track progress through dashboard insights
7. Review your financial health score + benchmarks
Product Direction Budgeting, forecasting, investing, net worth tracking, retirement planning, military/TSP support, shared couple budgeting, and AI financial coaching.
Privacy Direction Start local-first and manual-entry friendly. Sync, imports, and connected accounts should stay optional features later—not required.
Support & Help FAQ, troubleshooting, export/import help, privacy policy, terms, and support contact options will live here as the app moves beyond prototype stage.
Feature Roadmap Coming Soon:
• Receipt scanning
• AI coaching insights
• Retirement / FIRE calculator
• Spouse & shared budgeting
• Investment automation
• Subscription optimization
• Excel import + export improvements
