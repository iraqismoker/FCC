Budget Foundation Notes

What this first version is solving before we build the rest of the Budget tab.
Why Income + Fixed Bills first? These are the predictable pieces. Once they are entered, the app can estimate upcoming impact, bill load, and what is left before groceries, gas, dining out, and goals.
What comes next? Next phases should add variable categories, sinking funds, reimbursements, debt APR, emergency fund rules, and monthly/biweekly budget views.
